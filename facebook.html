Facebook is a social networking platform that allows users to connect with friends, family, and acquaintances online. It was founded by Mark Zuckerberg and his college roommates at Harvard University in 2004. Users can create profiles, share updates, photos, videos, and engage with content shared by others through likes, comments, and shares. Facebook also offers messaging features, event organization, and pages for businesses, organizations, and public figures.
