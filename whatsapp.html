𝐅𝐚𝐜𝐞𝐛𝐨𝐨𝐤            𝐆𝐨𝐨𝐠𝐥𝐞            𝐘𝐨𝐮𝐭𝐮𝐛𝐞            𝐓𝐰𝐢𝐭𝐭𝐞𝐫            𝐖𝐡𝐚𝐭𝐬𝐚𝐩𝐩





WhatsApp is a messaging application that allows users to send text messages, voice messages, make voice and video calls, share images, videos, documents, and other media securely over the internet. It was founded in 2009 by Brian Acton and Jan Koum, and later acquired by Facebook in 2014. WhatsApp is available for smartphones and desktop computers and is known for its end-to-end encryption, which helps ensure the privacy and security of user communication. It is widely used around the world as a convenient and reliable messaging platform.
