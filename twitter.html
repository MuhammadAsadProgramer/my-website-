Twitter is a social media platform that enables users to post and interact with messages known as "tweets." Tweets are limited to 280 characters and can include text, images, videos, links, and hashtags. Twitter allows users to follow other users to see their tweets in their feed, and users can also engage with tweets by liking, retweeting, replying, or quoting them. It is widely used for sharing thoughts, news, updates, opinions, and engaging in discussions on various topics in real-time. Twitter was founded in 2006 by Jack Dorsey, Biz Stone, and Evan Williams.
