𝐅𝐚𝐜𝐞𝐛𝐨𝐨𝐤            𝐆𝐨𝐨𝐠𝐥𝐞            𝐘𝐨𝐮𝐭𝐮𝐛𝐞            𝐓𝐰𝐢𝐭𝐭𝐞𝐫            𝐖𝐡𝐚𝐭𝐬𝐚𝐩𝐩





Google is a multinational technology company known primarily for its search engine, which allows users to search the internet for information, websites, images, videos, and more. Founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin while they were Ph.D. students at Stanford University, Google has since expanded its offerings to include a wide range of products and services, including Gmail (email service), Google Maps, Google Drive (cloud storage), Google Docs (document editing), Google Photos, YouTube, and the Android operating system for mobile devices, among others. Google's mission is to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful.
